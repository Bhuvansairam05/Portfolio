Clinic Management System


This system is mainly focused to handle the patients data under a clinic.
As we know that patients data plays a crucial role in a clinic and every
single data should be handled very carefully.


  • In this mainly consits of 3 types of logins.
  • 1st login is for doctor, 2nd login is for receptionist and 3rd login
    is for lab assistant
  • Doctor can access both patient op's and also lab records, whereas
    receptionist only handle op's and lab assistant only handles
    lab records of a specific patient.
  • Every patient is accessed uniquely using patient unique id.
